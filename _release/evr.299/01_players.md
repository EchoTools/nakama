## Release Notes v3.27.2-evr.299

Bug fix for party members getting split from their leader during matchmaking.

### Party System

* **Party Follow Fix:** Fixed a bug where party members could get separated from their leader when entering a match. The server was incorrectly detecting that a follower had joined the leader's match before the join was actually complete, causing the follower to stay behind in the lobby while the leader moved on.
